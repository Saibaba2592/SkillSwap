Welcome to SkillSwap

Connect, teach, and learn skills from people around the world.

Explore SkillSwap

SkillSwap is a peer-to-peer platform where people teach and learn from one another — no money involved! Users list skills they can teach (e.g., coding, cooking, yoga) and those they want to learn. Matches are made based on interests, availability, and proximity. After sessions, users rate each other to build a trusted network.

  • Free skill exchange system
  • Personalized matchmaking
  • Support for both online and offline sessions
  • Feedback system for quality assurance
